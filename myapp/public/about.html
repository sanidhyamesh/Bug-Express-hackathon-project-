About

GreenEx is a one of its kind venture in the voluntary carbon markets. The inception of it is to facilitate an open – market, free trading platform to facilitate players to be actively involved in carbon credits, CER’S and various other environment mitigation measures. The platform is unique in the sense that it takes away the hassle of verification of paperwork and other legal issues plaguing many industries to enter into the carbon trading market. Through its various features, the platform makes it beneficial for high performing industries to generate a source of revenue from carbon trading.



Vision


“ To catalyse a domestic voluntary carbon market in India and create an awareness among the people as well as the industry segments regarding their carbon requirements and footprint. ”
