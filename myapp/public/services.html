Our Services

We provide best services

Buy :


Developed countries who wish to offset their carbon levels and play an important role in technology transfer (under CDM) can purchase the Emission Reductions (ER’s) / Carbon Credits or allowances under the national trading system in real time on the platform.


Sell :


Countries who are growing their economy and capacity can in their early growth try and reduce their carbon levels by adopting green measures and methodologies. This process from initial stage will be taken care of and credits will be traded at regular intervals providing adequate incentive in the form of allowances.


Compliances / Legal Procedures :


With the issue of carbon credits being awarded by the government and trading it in real – time and full value in terms of future – exchanges will need to hold certain credibility in terms of generation of emission reductions. Current mechanisms suggest that two – step verification / authentication is needed in order to generated a trust – based relationship between buyer and seller. A systematic approach using the help of designated authorities and our team will help bridge this gap.


Pathway to CDM / JI :

For new industries or late entrants into the carbon market, a complete hand-holding approach to fulfil the green objectives of the industry involving realisation of potential, purchasing of technology, effective paper-work / monitoring mechanism, etc. will be provided.
